Operational Infrastructure

Laboratory Services

Built through years of deliberate investment in capacity and infrastructure, Rinda Ubuzima's research clinics and laboratories in Kigali are equipped to support the full demands of rigorous clinical trials. The clinic maintains core emergency and monitoring equipment including defibrillators, ECG, and emergency trolleys, while the laboratory holds advanced diagnostic and analytical capacity — including a hematology analyzer, full automated biochemistry analyzer, Gene Xpert machine, Eppendorf centrifuge, and ultra-cold storage — making it a well-resourced and credible implementation partner for complex research studies.

Laboratory Services

Rinda Ubuzima's Kigali-based clinic and laboratory are the product of sustained, strategic investment and purpose-built to support clinical trials from first patient visit to final sample analysis. With diagnostic and analytical infrastructure spanning hematology, biochemistry, infectious disease testing, and cold chain management, the facility speaks for itself as a site equipped for serious science.

What this includes

  • Research laboratory support in Kigali
  • Study-linked sample handling and workflow support
  • Equipment-enabled research operations
  • Quality-oriented laboratory processes
  • Integration with clinic and study implementation
