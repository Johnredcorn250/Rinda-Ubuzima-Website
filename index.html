About

A clearer institutional story, without losing authority.

This redesign keeps your existing color theme, but improves hierarchy, readability, responsiveness, and usability so the website feels more professional and easier to navigate on all screen sizes.

This version simplifies the visual weight of the site while preserving the strong red identity already established in your baseline design.

The layout is more flexible, the navigation works on mobile, and the content structure is now more interoperable. Instead of repeating static HTML blocks, the news, team, gallery, partners, and careers sections can now be updated through compact data arrays.

That means future edits are easier, cleaner, and faster, especially when the site starts carrying real announcements, publications, people, vacancies, or campaign material.
