Overview

Capacity Building

Capacity building is part of Rinda Ubuzima’s stated mission and public profile. The organisation presents training, mentorship, and collaboration as part of how it contributes to stronger research systems and public health practice in Rwanda and the region.

What this includes

  • Training for health workers and research teams
  • Mentorship and peer-to-peer learning
  • Support for stronger research systems
  • Collaboration around Africa-led research readiness
  • Knowledge-sharing through institutional partnerships
Why It Matters

Capability that supports trusted delivery

Long-term value

Capacity building helps ensure programmes leave stronger systems behind, not just short-term activity outputs.

Partner confidence

Organizations that train and mentor locally are often better partners for sustainable implementation and research growth.

Regional relevance

Recent collaboration updates point to a clear interest in peer learning, harmonisation, and sustainable capacity across African partners.
