Clinical Research

Rinda Ubuzima conducts and supports ethical clinical research in Rwanda, with experience spanning HIV, reproductive health, COVID-19, malaria, and other priority health areas. Its public profiles describe a research clinic and laboratory in Kigali and a long-standing focus on clinical trials and implementation research.

What this includes

  • Protocol implementation and site coordination
  • Participant recruitment, retention, and follow-up
  • Research clinic operations in Kigali
  • Support for vaccine, diagnostic, and therapeutic studies
  • Collaboration with national and international research partners
